Experiment 020 — Cursor-Only Learning

Date: 2026-02-23  |  Duration: 0.8s

Hypothesis

Global reward works if you only change a few synapses at a time. A cursor restricts perturbation to a local spatial neighbourhood — one scalar reward is enough to evaluate that small local change. Like a genetic algorithm running inside a network.
